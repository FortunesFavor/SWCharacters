Powers

Technometry - 
Out of Combat: Able to sense the inner workings of machines, and even perform simple 'mind-tricks' on Droids. It also allows remote activation of switches and levers and similar
In Combat: Able to stun droids, or disable devices and weapons for 1 round for every 5 stamina spent

Telekenesis - 
Out of Combat: Able to lift, pull, push, and move around up to 200Lbs
In Combat: Spending 10 stamina allows you to lift, pull, push, and move around up to 200Lbs, as an attack it deals one shift of damage ignoring armor

Lightsaber Deflect -
In Combat: Spend 10 stamina to deflect a bolt and gain an attack roll to use the deflected bolt as a weapon.
